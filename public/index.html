The Fragrances
All dopaminé fragrances are Parfum — the highest concentration available in fine fragrance, typically 20–40% aromatic compounds. This means deeper sillage, greater longevity, and a more intimate evolution on skin compared to lighter concentrations. We do not produce Eau de Parfum or Eau de Toilette.
Yes. dopaminé is a unisex house. Every fragrance in our collection is crafted to be worn by anyone — our philosophy is that scent belongs to the person, not a gender. Explore what moves you.
A fragrance unfolds in three acts. Top notes are the opening impression — what you smell in the first moments. Heart notes emerge as the top fades, forming the true character of the scent. Base notes are the foundation — the deep, lasting warmth that lingers on your skin for hours after application. Parfum concentrations give the base notes particular richness and depth.
No — and that is precisely what makes Parfum extraordinary. Your skin chemistry, body temperature, diet, and even hydration all influence how a fragrance develops. The same bottle can smell subtly different on two people. This is not a flaw — it is the intimacy of fine fragrance. What you wear becomes yours alone.
Application — Spray lightly on pulse points: inner wrists, neck, behind ears, inner elbows. Do not rub. Allow the fragrance to dry and settle naturally on your skin.

Storage — Keep your bottle away from direct sunlight, heat, and humidity. Store upright in a cool, dry place — ideally in its original box. Proper storage preserves the integrity of the fragrance.
The Discovery Set
The Discovery Set contains five 15ml bottles — one of each fragrance in our current collection. It is designed for those who wish to experience every mood before committing to a full bottle. Each 15ml is a true Parfum concentration, not a diluted sample.
Absolutely. At Parfum concentration, 15ml goes a significant distance. A single spray is all that is needed for most occasions. The Discovery Set gives you enough of each fragrance to wear it across different settings, weather, and moods — which is exactly how a fine Parfum reveals itself over time.
Orders & Shipping
Yes. dopaminé ships worldwide. Regardless of where you are, we will get your order to you. Shipping costs and availability are calculated at checkout based on your location.
Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to follow your shipment in real time through our courier's tracking portal. If you have not received tracking information after placing your order, please contact us and we will assist you immediately.
We currently accept Bank Transfer. After placing your order, you will receive payment details via email. Online card payments will be available shortly.
Yes. All orders are handled securely and your personal information is never shared with third parties. Our bottles are carefully packaged to withstand transit — each order is treated with the same care as the fragrance inside it.
Returns & Refunds
Due to the nature of fine Parfum, we do not accept returns on opened or used products. We make exceptions in two cases only:

1. You received a product that is entirely different from what you ordered.
2. The product arrives completely sealed and unused — in its original, unopened condition.

If either of these applies to you, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order with your order number and photographs of the item.
If your order arrives damaged in transit, please contact us immediately with photographs of the packaging and the product. We will assess each case individually and work to make it right. We take the condition of every delivery seriously.
